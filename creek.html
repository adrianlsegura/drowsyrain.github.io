About

This site serves as a homebrew guide to identifying the native and invasive flora that paint the area around Strawberry Creek on campus. The locations are limited to the parts of Strawberry Creek that intertwine with the UC Berkeley campus. Note that this is not an extensive guide and the images and descriptions of the plants may differ depending on the season.


If there is something you'd like to see added or if there's any inaccuracies please reach out to me.
